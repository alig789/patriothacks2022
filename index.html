Refugee Reunification Services

Over 2.3 million refugees have left Ukraine since the Russian invasion began. As men are conscripted to defend their nation, nearly all Ukranian families are now separated.

Among these refugees are women, children, and elderly who may have limited contact with their family members.

This service is designed for refugees who have lost contact with their families entirely. Through Autosomal DNA testing, we can identify and reunite separated family members.
